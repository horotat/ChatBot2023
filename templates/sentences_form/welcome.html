Welcome

You are going to speak to a artificial conversation partner with the purpose of practicing your English.
The agent will take on a tutoring role and sometimes correct any mistakes you make.

Instructions:

  • The conversational agent is powered by GPT-3. Initially you receive a message from the agent and you need to reply.
  • Try to respond quickly and intuitively, you are encouraged to not worry about making mistakes
  • For each reply that you receive from the system, you need to rate the reply with "good" or "bad". For that you push the buttons G or B on your keyboard. You should evaluate the response based on if it is generally adequate and human-like or if you for example find it annoying.
  • At some points you will be prompted by the agent to start a new conversation. When prompted, press "new conversation" at the bottom of the screen. After that point the agent is not aware of your previous conversation.

GDPR

  • The conversation you have with the chatbot is saved. If you do not want real answers from you saved, feel free to answer with an imaginary response.
  • The saved conversation is anonymized and no personal information about you is saved.
